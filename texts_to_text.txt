=====================================================================
 TEST TRANSCRIPTS — realistic two-speaker conversations, UNLABELED
=====================================================================
How to use: open http://localhost:3000, pick the matching language
(English section -> "en", Armenian section -> "hy"), paste ONE whole
example (all its lines together) into the transcript box, click
Analyze.

Format of every example below:
  - TWO speakers (an operator and a caller), but NEITHER is labeled
    ("Agent:" / "Caller:" do NOT appear anywhere) — exactly like a
    real transcript with no diarization tags.
  - One utterance per line. This is what lets the backend's fallback
    logic alternate roles correctly (first line = Agent, then it
    alternates) even though nothing in the text says who is who.
  - The personal info is NOT dumped in one sentence anymore — it's
    spread across several of the caller's separate answers, which is
    how a real call actually unfolds (name in one answer, address in
    another, phone in another, etc.).
  - Not every conversation reveals every attribute — some are missing
    a phone, or an SSN, etc., on purpose, to also check that missing
    attributes correctly come back as null instead of being guessed.
